Dog Hunting

A tale of two dogs in the Meetup office.

Meetup loves dogs; so much so that they end up wandering around the office all day. Now we have a better idea of the migratory habits of Doug and Lola. This research was made possible through an ingenious bluetooth device, dreamed up by Dr. Richard Hsieh of Meetup University.
